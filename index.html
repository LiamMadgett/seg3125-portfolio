About Me

Hi! My name is Liam Madgett. I recently completed my third year of Software Engineering at the uOttawa. I’ve previously worked as a co-op student in the field of embedded software, where I focused on low level system programming and hardware interaction. Now, I’m excited to explore the world of UI design, as it offers a more user-facing perspective, allowing me to create experiences that directly impact how people interact with tech.

How I Work

I'm currently learning about user interface design principles through SEG3125 "Designing User Interfaces" at uOttawa. As part of this course, I'm gaining hands on experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, while also exploring key design concepts like visual hierarchy, balance, contrast, and user-centered design.

You can find the course materials here: SEG3125 Brightspace Course Page .
